    Ty's Power

    Dustin takes off

    "Now, Dustin, we're going to go home and see June and Mark again," Ty said with a sinister laugh, "See if they are behaving."

    Dustin looked at Ty, about to show some attitude, when he thought again, and just nodded. They walked towards the exit when they suddenly heard footsteps behind
    them. It was Cindy. She came to them, "I'm glad I found you guys before you left the mall!" she said in her airhead voice, "Can I get a ride home with you guys? I
    came with Mark, but Katie can't seem to find him."

    Dustin was about to refuse, not wanting his girlfriend to see him this way, but Ty put his hand on Dustin's shoulder, and said, "Sure, he'd be happy to drive us home."
    Dustin shot Ty a look of dislike to which Ty only grinned.

    Dustin, Ty, and Cindy walked out to the car. Dustin was about to get in when Ty walked to the driver's side, "Dustin, your seat fell back! Let me adjust it for you!" He
    moved the seat up to where he thought Dustin should have it now. Cindy giggled slightly at Ty and got into the back seat, allowing Ty to sit next to Dustin in the front.

    Cindy and Ty talked all the way to her house, Cindy barely acknowledging Dustin at all. As they pulled in, Cindy gave Ty a hug from the back seat, giving Dustin only
    a small, "Thank you." before getting out and heading into her house. Dustin pulled out of her driveway and started for their house.

    "Pull into this gas station, Dustin. We need to get some food that I like around the house. If you are good, I'll let you have a burrito or something to take home," Ty said
    as Dustin sighed and pulled into the station. Ty got out of the car as Dustin sat in the car. Then realizing it was his only chance, Dustin sped off with the car down the
    highway leaving Ty in the station by himself.
